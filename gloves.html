A glove (Middle English from Old English glof) is a garment covering the
whole hand. Gloves usually have separate sheaths or openings for each
finger and the thumb.If there is an opening but no (or a short) covering
sheath for each finger they are called fingerless gloves. Fingerless gloves
having one large opening rather than individual openings for each finger
are sometimes called gauntlets, though gauntlets are not necessarily
fingerless.
